About Me

Hi! I'm Ashe Gravenkemper, and I recently moved to Dallas to start my college journey. Alongside my studies, I work as a senior technician at a gaming company, where I apply my technical skills in the industry. I also completed the Harvard CS50 Web Design program, which strengthened my passion for web development. My goal is to become a web developer, creating engaging and user-friendly websites that blend creativity with functionality.

My Hobbies

One of my biggest passions is playing the bass. I love the deep, powerful sound and how it lays the foundation of a song. Whether I'm learning new riffs or just jamming, it's my favorite way to express myself—especially with punk music, which has had a huge influence on me.

I also have a creative side when it comes to hair. Dyeing, bleaching, and cutting hair is something I genuinely enjoy. It's exciting to mix colors, experiment with different styles, and help people achieve a look that makes them feel more confident. It's like an art form, and I love seeing the transformations.

In my free time, I like to unwind with League of Legends. Whether I'm playing competitively or just for fun, I enjoy the teamwork, strategy, and challenge the game offers. Gaming is also a great way to connect with friends and meet new people.

Another big interest of mine is urbex—urban exploration. There's something fascinating about discovering abandoned places, seeing what's been left behind, and capturing the atmosphere through photography. Every location has a unique history, and I love the adventure of exploring hidden or forgotten spaces.

At the heart of all my hobbies is my love for meeting new people. Whether it's through music, gaming, or shared experiences, I enjoy making connections and learning from others. Life is about growth and discovery, and I'm always looking for new ways to explore the world around me.
